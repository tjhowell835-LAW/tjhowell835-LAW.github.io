About this space

Bixby Cove Cottage is a spacious 1,200 sq ft lake‑front retreat on Lake Eufaula. Four cozy bedrooms and two full bathrooms provide space for families and groups. The open concept living area flows into a fully remodeled kitchen stocked with cooking essentials, while the large dining table converts to a pool and ping‑pong table for evening fun. After a day on the lake, unwind on the extended front porch swing or gather around the fire pit under the stars. Self check‑in and a quiet lakeside location make your stay easy and peaceful. Read on to learn about the sleeping arrangements, amenities and what makes our home unique.
